Our Services

At SEALANDER Crewing Agency, we provide a full range of services to support shipowners and seafarers, ensuring reliable and efficient crew management. Our core offerings include:

Recruitment and Selection of Ukrainian Seafarers
We specialize in sourcing and recruiting skilled Ukrainian seafarers across all ranks. Each candidate is carefully vetted to ensure they meet international standards, possess the necessary certifications, and have the experience to excel in their roles.
Consulting Services for Shipowners
Our consulting services are designed to support shipowners in optimizing their operations. We provide expert advice on crew management, compliance with international maritime regulations, and best practices for safe and efficient vessel operations.
Assistance with Maritime Documents and Certifications
We help seafarers obtain and renew essential maritime documents and certifications, including:
• STCW 78/95 compliance certificates
• Flag state endorsements
• Medical fitness certificates
• Other necessary documentation required for international assignments
Our team ensures a seamless process, saving time and effort for seafarers.
Comprehensive Crew Management Solutions
We offer tailored crew management services to meet the specific needs of each vessel. From pre-deployment briefings to familiarizing crews with client policies, we ensure smooth integration and operational readiness. We are dedicated to delivering exceptional service and fostering strong partnerships with our clients. Whether you are a shipowner in need of reliable crews or a seafarer seeking professional support, we are here to help.
